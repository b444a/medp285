Who are the Slushy Noobz?

The Slushy Noobz are a Canadian YouTube channel created by Hamzah and Martin, known for their comedic gaming videos, vlogs, and mukbangs. The channel was launched in May 2023, with their first video, "We beat the enderdragon," released on May 31, 2023. They have also established a significant presence on Instagram, where they share updates and engage with their audience.

The Impact

Martin and Hamzah have significantly impacted Generation Z by reviving the authentic and personal feel of early YouTube content. Their videos have a nostalgic charm reminiscent of the platform's beginnings, focusing on genuine creativity over commercialism. This approach resonates deeply with Gen Z viewers, who appreciate content that feels sincere and relatable. Slushy Noobz live-streamed a boxing match titled "Hamzah vs Martin," which garnered over 1.1 million views. The stream featured both the weigh-in and the main event, captivating their audience with this unique content. Their innovative content and genuine connection with their audience have positioned Slushy Noobz as influential figures among Gen Z, contributing to a resurgence of interest in YouTube's long-form content.

What is the Slushy Virus?
